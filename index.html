Winter is coming!

Experience the thrills and no frills action of the Thunder Bay Ski scene!

Thunder Bay, Ontario, Canada is a beautiful place to visit in the winter.More

e city is one of Canada’s best winter destinations. It is surrounded by the impressive Nor’wester mountains, which provide a beautiful backdrop to the city and world-class ski facilities. The winters in Thunder Bay can be quite chilly and snowy, but that is exactly why the city is a great place to visit in the winter. There are many outdoor activities to enjoy, such as skiing, snowboarding, snowmobiling, snowshoeing, ice climbing, ice fishing, and even snowkiting.
