Winter is coming!

Experience the thrills and no frills action of the Thunder Bay Ski scene!

Certainly! Thunder Bay, Ontario, Canada is a beautiful place to visit in the winter. According to the Thunder Bay Tourism website, the city is one of Canada’s best winter destinations1. The city is surrounded by the impressive Nor’wester mountains, which provide a beautiful backdrop to the city and world-class ski facilities1. The winters in Thunder Bay can be quite chilly and snowy, but that is exactly why the city is a great place to visit in the winter. There are many outdoor activities to enjoy, such as skiing, snowboarding, snowmobiling, snowshoeing, ice climbing, ice fishing, and even snowkiting12.
